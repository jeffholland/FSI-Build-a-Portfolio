My Projects

Here is a demonstration of my thesis project for the Music Technology Master's program at NYU: a computer music interface developed in C++ using the JUCE library. The project is based on connecting objects known as "modules" in order to produce sounds. The goal is to create an environment of high flexibility, a kind of free-reign music creation sandbox.

Read the thesis paper itself here.

Here is a demonstration of one of my music projects: an "unstable drum machine" developed in Pure Data (Pd). "Unstable" means that certain random variables affect parameters such as the speed/pitch of sample playback, the length of each note, and the sample selection itself.
